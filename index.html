ABOUT US

About AimNxt Technologies

A Hyderabad-based cyber security training institute focused on one outcome — turning beginners into hired SOC Analysts.

AimNxt Technologies is a Hyderabad-based cyber security training institute built around a single outcome: preparing students for real jobs as SOC Analysts. Instead of long theory-heavy syllabuses, the training is structured around the tools, workflows and incident scenarios that security teams handle every day.

Every batch is kept practical and mentor-led, combining live SIEM labs, threat simulations and incident investigation exercises with resume building, mock interviews and placement support — so students leave with a portfolio of hands-on work, not just a certificate.

Whether you're a fresher, a career switcher, or an IT professional moving into security, the program is designed to take you from fundamentals to job-ready in three months.
